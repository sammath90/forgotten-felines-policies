# GDPR & Data Protection Policy — Volunteers / General Group Chats

**Purpose:** To ensure volunteers communicate safely and responsibly in group chats, protecting personal information of adopters, the public, and fellow volunteers.

---

## 1. General Principles

Volunteers should:

- Be mindful that group chats are not private.
- Share only information necessary for rescue activities.
- Avoid posting personal or sensitive information.

## 2. What Not to Post

Volunteers must not post:

**Personal Data:**
- Names, phone numbers, email addresses
- Addresses or location information
- Screenshots of private conversations
- Personal circumstances of adopters or supporters

**Images & Media:**
- Photos of children
- Photos revealing home environments without consent
- Images showing house numbers, car registrations, or identifiable people

## 3. Careful Use of Photos

- Ensure no identifiable people appear in the background.
- Do not show private homes or belongings without permission.
- Do not share adopter images without consent.

## 4. Respecting Volunteer Privacy

- Do not share volunteer phone numbers or private messages without permission.
- Avoid discussing personal matters in group chats.

## 5. Group Chat Conduct

- Keep discussions rescue-related.
- Avoid gossip or commentary about adopters or volunteers.
- If unsure, send the content privately to a trustee first.

## 6. Reporting Concerns

Any privacy issue should be reported to a trustee immediately.
