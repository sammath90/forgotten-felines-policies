# Cat Intake & Medical Care Policy

**Forgotten Felines Cat Rescue — Charity No. 1181190**

---

- All cats must be added to the "Current Cats" Google spreadsheet immediately upon entering care.
- The receiving fosterer is responsible for creating the cat's entry.
- Flea, worming, and all other treatments must be recorded on the Medical Record Sheet.
- Partner vets identify each cat on invoices using First Name + Fosterer's Surname.
- Full veterinary records are maintained by partner vets and available on request.
- If a cat has received medical treatment, the adopter must be provided with relevant medical notes at handover.
- A cat may only be rehomed once a partner vet has confirmed it is fit to home.
- All cats are neutered at 4 months of age.
- Neutering may be delayed only on veterinary advice, such as illness or being underweight.

---

## Responsibilities

**Fosterers:** Maintain accurate records, administer routine treatments, and provide adopters with medical notes.

**Partner Vets:** Provide treatment, maintain medical histories, and confirm fitness for rehoming.

**Coordinators:** Ensure compliance and maintain access to required forms.

---

*This policy will be reviewed annually.*
