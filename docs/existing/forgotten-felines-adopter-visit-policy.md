# Forgotten Felines – Adopter Visit Policy

**Purpose:** This policy ensures safe, respectful, and GDPR-compliant visits when adopters meet cats in foster homes.

---

## 1. Consent & Privacy

- No photos or videos may be taken without explicit permission from the fosterer.
- If consent is given, photos may only include the cat(s) being visited.
- Photos must not include people, children, other animals, or identifiable parts of the home.
- Livestreaming of any kind is not permitted.

## 2. Conduct During the Visit

- Adopters must follow the fosterer's instructions at all times.
- Visits must remain within the room designated by the fosterer.
- Doors to other rooms must not be opened.
- Cat handling is only allowed with permission.

## 3. Safety & Respect

- If the fosterer asks the adopter to leave, they must leave immediately.
- Rough handling or discipline of the cat is prohibited.
- Noise levels must be kept low to avoid stressing the animals.

## 4. Children

- Children must be supervised at all times.
- Children should remain calm and seated unless advised otherwise.
- The fosterer may end the visit if safety becomes a concern.

## 5. Personal Belongings

- Bags should remain closed and out of reach of cats.
- No food, treats, or toys may be given without permission.

## 6. Data Protection

- Any information seen or heard in the foster home must remain private.
- Adopters must not share details about the home, the fosterer, or other cats.
