# Forgotten Felines – Fosterer Visit Policy

**Purpose:** This policy provides guidance for fosterers hosting adopter visits to ensure safety, boundaries, and GDPR compliance.

---

## 1. Preparing for the Visit

- Choose one safe, contained room for the visit.
- Ensure other animals are separated.
- Remove or hide any personal documents or identifiable items.

## 2. Consent for Photos

- State clearly whether photos are allowed.
- Only allow photos of the cat being visited.
- Do not allow photos of yourself, children, or identifiable belongings.
- Withdraw consent if needed.

## 3. Managing the Visit

- Explain the cat's temperament and handling rules.
- Supervise the visit at all times.
- Keep adopters within the designated room.
- Do not allow access to other rooms or areas of the home.

## 4. Ending a Visit

You may end a visit immediately if:

- Instructions are ignored
- A child's behaviour becomes unsafe
- The cat shows signs of stress
- The adopter is inappropriate or aggressive
- You feel uncomfortable for any reason

If asked to leave, adopters must leave immediately.

## 5. Safety & Wellbeing

- Do not permit picking up unless safe for the cat.
- Ensure doors and windows remain secure.
- Never leave adopters alone with the cat.

## 6. Data Protection

- Share only the minimum personal information needed.
- Remove documents with sensitive details before visits.
- Do not allow adopters to photograph anything except the cat.
