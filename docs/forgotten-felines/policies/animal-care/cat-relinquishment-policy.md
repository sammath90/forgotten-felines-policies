# Cat Relinquishment Policy

**Forgotten Felines Cat Rescue — Charity No. 1181190**

---

## Stray Cats

Before accepting a stray cat into our care, all reasonable steps will be taken to determine whether the cat has an owner. These steps include:

- Checking for a microchip
- Advertising the found cat on social media platforms
- Distributing leaflets in the local area if possible
- Displaying posters in prominent locations if possible
- Listing the cat on lost and found pet websites
- Using a paper collar to identify potential ownership
- Encouraging the finder to make enquiries locally, including knocking on doors and speaking with neighbours

Stray cats will only be made available for rehoming after:

- A minimum holding period of **7 days** in our care has elapsed
- The cat has undergone a veterinary health check
- The cat has been microchipped, neutered, and vaccinated, unless otherwise advised by a veterinary professional

---

## Surrendered Cats

If a cat is surrendered to us, the relevant surrender form passing ownership to Forgotten Felines must be completed by the current owner. This form must be passed to a Trustee.

---

## Note

If a cat has a microchip where the original owner cannot be contacted, or the microchip has not been registered, the cat will not be rehomed for **28 days**.

---

*This policy will be reviewed annually.*
