## Purpose

This checklist is the documented assessment artefact required by ADCH Standard 60. No foster home may be used to house a Forgotten Felines cat until this checklist has been completed in full and the home has been formally approved, approved-with-conditions, or declined by the Fostering Coordinator.

A completed checklist is retained alongside the signed Fostering Agreement. The Foster Home Register (maintained by the Fostering Coordinator) records every approved home and its next review date.

---

## When to use this checklist

- **Initial assessment** — before a foster home is used for the first time.
- **Annual review** — at least once every 12 months.
- **Triggered re-assessment** — whenever any of the following occur:
  - Change of address
  - New resident pet, child, or adult joining the household
  - Any welfare incident involving a foster cat in the home
  - A gap of more than 12 months in the fosterer's training record
  - Resident pet vaccination status lapses

---

## Assessment method

- **In person** is preferred and must happen at least every 12 months.
- **Virtual** (video call + photographs + floor-plan sketch) is permitted for the initial assessment where an in-person visit is impractical, provided that an in-person follow-up takes place within 12 months.
- The Fostering Coordinator (or a Trustee delegated by them) is the assessor. The assessor must be trained in the full Fosterer Training Programme and must not be related to or living in the prospective foster home.

## Retention

A completed and signed checklist is retained for the duration of the fosterer's active engagement plus 12 months thereafter, in line with the Data Retention Policy. Checklists are stored securely alongside the Fostering Agreement and the fosterer's training record.
