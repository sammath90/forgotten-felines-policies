## 1. Purpose

This register is the master log of every foster home approved to care for Forgotten Felines cats. It is the operational output of the [Foster Home Assessment Checklist](foster-home-assessment-checklist.md) and the documented evidence required by ADCH Minimum Welfare Operational Standard 60.

A cat may not be placed in any foster home that does not have a current, active row in this register with an approved status.

The register is maintained by the Fostering Coordinator. Trustees review it at least annually and it is audited as part of the Phase 1 compliance check.

## 3. Status definitions

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Active** | Approved, current assessment, Fostering Agreement signed, training up to date, and eligible to take new placements |
| **Paused** | Temporarily not taking placements (e.g. fosterer illness, bereavement, household change) but remains approved. A re-activation check is required before the next placement |
| **Retired** | No longer fostering. Record retained for audit and welfare-history purposes in line with the Data Retention Policy |
| **Declined** | Assessed but not approved, or approval withdrawn. Record retained with the reason |

---

## 4. Triggered re-assessment

A re-assessment is triggered (and logged in the Triggered re-assessment history field) whenever any of the following occur:

- A change of address
- A new resident pet, child, or adult joining the household
- Any welfare incident involving a foster cat in that home
- A gap of more than 12 months in the fosterer's training record
- Resident pet vaccination status lapses
- The Fostering Coordinator's judgement

The re-assessment uses the Foster Home Assessment Checklist.
