# Phase 1 Finalisation Checklist — Forgotten Felines Cat Rescue

**Charity No.:** 1181190  
**Compiled:** 2026-04-12  
**Owner:** Sam Mathias  
**Purpose:** Consolidated list of everything needed before the Phase 1 policy suite can be trustee-approved and published.

Items are grouped by theme and sequenced so that blockers appear first. Each item is tagged with the compliance area(s) it serves.

## Legend

| Tag | Area |
|---|---|
| `ADCH` | ADCH Minimum Welfare & Operational Standards |

## Block A — Role Assignments (Must be resolved first)

These are hard blockers. Policies with unresolved escalation paths cannot be published.

- [ ] **Assign Lottery Licence Holder** — must match the named individual on the active Gambling Act licence. Confirm with Bev who holds this. `ADCH` `CC`
- [ ] **Assign Cat Intake Lead** — needed for the intake procedure (ADCH Standards 1–4). `ADCH`
- [ ] **Assign Isolation Coordinator** — can be the Vet Liaison (Becca James); just needs to be formally documented. `ADCH`
- [ ] **Assign Behavioural Support Contact** — can be an external behaviourist; name and contact details needed (ADCH Standard 55). `ADCH`

---

## Block B — Missing Policies (Must be drafted before trustee approval)

Policies that are required but do not yet exist as drafts.

- [ ] **Consolidated Cats in Care Policy** — combine `cat-intake-policy.md`, `cat-isolation-arrival-policy.md`, and `fostering-policy.md` into a single ADCH-aligned policy. The three individual drafts can remain as supporting documents. `ADCH`
- [ ] **Safeguarding Policy** — must include statutory escalation steps (referral to police/social services). Required by Charity Commission and ADCH Standard 78. `ADCH` `CC`
- [ ] **Incident Reporting & Complaints Policy** — covers both internal complaints and Serious Incident Reporting to the Charity Commission. `CC`

---

## Block D — Publish and Distribute

Actions once trustee approval is complete.

- [ ] **Publish Privacy Notice on the website** — must be live before any public data collection continues. `GDPR`
- [ ] **Circulate Roles & Responsibilities Register** to all trustees and foster coordinators. `ADCH` `CC`
- [ ] **Issue published `.docx` copies** of all approved policies. `ADCH` `CC` `GDPR`
- [ ] **Obtain signed acknowledgements** from all trustees (Trustee Code of Conduct, GDPR Trustee Policy). `CC` `GDPR`
- [ ] **Obtain signed acknowledgements** from all active fosterers (Fostering Policy, GDPR Volunteer Policy, Volunteer Policy). `ADCH` `GDPR`

---

## Block E — Documentation and Evidence

Records needed to evidence compliance — not policies, but operational artefacts.

- [ ] **Complete nominated vet details** — fill in phone numbers and primary contact names in Roles & Responsibilities Register Section 5. `ADCH`
- [ ] **Document isolation arrangement with vet** — written confirmation (email is fine) that the nominated vet can take in an infectious cat if needed (ADCH Standard 13). `ADCH`
- [ ] **Per-cat record log** — one record per cat: description, intake date, vet history, assessments, adopter details. A Google Sheet is sufficient (ADCH Standard 76). `ADCH`
- [ ] **Fosterer training records** — individual log of who was trained, on which of the 8 mandatory topics, and when (ADCH Standard 48). `ADCH`
- [ ] **Foster home assessment records** — one record per approved foster home confirming it was visited (ADCH Standard 60). `ADCH`
- [ ] **Conflicts of interest register** — maintained by the Chair; annual declaration required. `CC`
- [ ] **Confirm ICO fee registration status** — check whether an annual data protection fee is required and up to date. `GDPR`

---

## Block F — Fosterer Training Programme

Required by ADCH Standard 48. Must cover all 8 mandatory topics with individual records kept.

- [ ] Write or adopt a fosterer training programme covering:
  - Animal welfare and recognising poor welfare
  - Animal handling
  - Animal behaviour
  - Cleanliness and hygiene
  - Feeding and food preparation
  - Disease control
  - Recognising and responding to sick animals (first aid action)
  - New legislation, disease information, and inherited conditions
- [ ] Set a schedule for annual review of training content. `ADCH`

---

## Block G — ADCH Application

Once Blocks A–F are substantially complete.

- [ ] **Complete the ADCH self-assessment** against all applicable standards (SOP1, Step 3). `ADCH`
- [ ] **Gather application materials:**
  - 2 references (existing ADCH members or professionals — your vet qualifies)
  - Organisational constitution
  - 2 years of financial accounts or bank statements
- [ ] **Submit application** — email [email protected] or use the online form. `ADCH`

---

## Summary: Critical Path

The minimum viable path to trustee approval and publication is:

```
Block A (assign remaining roles)
  → Block B (draft missing policies)
    → Block C (trustee approval session)
      → Block D (publish + distribute)
```

Blocks E, F, and G can run in parallel with B and C but must be complete before the ADCH application (G) is submitted.
