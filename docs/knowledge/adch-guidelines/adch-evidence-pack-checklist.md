# ADCH Evidence Pack Checklist

**Prepared:** 2026-04-11  
**Purpose:** Define the operational evidence needed in addition to policy documents for ADCH application and assessment.

---

## 1. Governance and Role Ownership

- [ ] Current roles register with no critical `[TBC]` roles
- [ ] Named Safeguarding Lead
- [ ] Named Vet Liaison
- [ ] Named Cat Intake Lead
- [ ] Named Lottery Licence Holder
- [ ] Named Crisis / Media Contact

Primary source:

- `docs/forgotten-felines/registers/roles-and-responsibilities-register.md`

---

## 2. Policy Set (Current and Aligned)

- [ ] Consolidated Cats in Care policy (aligned to intake + isolation + fostering controls)
- [ ] Fosterer Cleaning policy
- [ ] Transportation policy
- [ ] Escape policy
- [ ] Death of a Cat policy
- [ ] Euthanasia policy
- [ ] Adoption policy
- [ ] Fostering policy
- [ ] Cat Relinquishment policy

Primary source:

- `docs/forgotten-felines/policies/animal-care/`

---

## 3. Veterinary and Isolation Evidence

- [ ] Nominated vet practices list complete (name, address, phone, primary contact)
- [ ] Written isolation arrangement confirmation (email/letter acceptable)
- [ ] Vet liaison role assignment and escalation route confirmed

Primary source:

- `docs/forgotten-felines/registers/roles-and-responsibilities-register.md`
- Related supporting file location to create: `docs/forgotten-felines/assessments/` (or `archive/source-documents/`)

---

## 4. Training and Competence Records

- [ ] Fosterer training programme covering all mandatory topic areas
- [ ] Per-person training log (who, what, when, refresher due date)
- [ ] Annual review date and owner for training content

Suggested record location:

- `docs/forgotten-felines/assessments/` (or future `records/` directory)

---

## 5. Animal and Home Records

- [ ] Per-cat record log (intake, treatment, assessments, outcome)
- [ ] Foster home assessment records (including Annex B suitability evidence)
- [ ] Incident records (escape, death, serious welfare concerns)

Suggested record location:

- `docs/forgotten-felines/assessments/` (or future `records/` directory)

---

## 6. ADCH Application Pack

- [ ] Completed ADCH self-assessment
- [ ] Two references
- [ ] Constitution
- [ ] Two years of accounts or bank statements
- [ ] Submission record and correspondence trail

Reference process docs:

- `docs/knowledge/adch-guidelines/sop1-new-member-applications.md`
- `docs/knowledge/adch-guidelines/sop2-existing-member-self-assessment.md`
- `docs/knowledge/adch-guidelines/sop3-existing-member-external-assessment.md`
