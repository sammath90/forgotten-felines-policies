# SOP 2: Existing Member Self-Assessment

**Source:** https://adch.org.uk/minimum-standards/existing-member-self-assessment-standard-operating-procedure-sop-2/
**Organisation:** Association of Dogs and Cats Homes (ADCH)

---

## Overview

This document outlines ADCH's process for member self-assessments, designed with transparency in mind for both members and applicants.

---

## Key Definitions

**Site/Centre:** A single location or group of locations housing animals under common management, which may or may not be owned by the rescue.

**Foster Carer:** Typically a domestic home providing temporary animal care. Animals may be housed in the home or purpose-built facilities on private property.

**SAWC:** Standards and Animal Welfare Committee, which holds delegated authority from Trustees for membership decisions.

---

## Assessment Procedure

1. All members undergo self-assessment every other year as requested by the Association.

2. External assessments may occur during self-assessment years or between them.

3. Members are exempt from self-assessments when an external assessment occurred within 6 months prior or a committed assessment falls within 6 weeks after the deadline.

4. Assessment forms and FAQs are distributed to members when due, with advance notice at meetings or conferences.

5. Members receive 8 weeks to complete assessments with designated points of contact.

6. Non-compliance areas require notation and remedial plans within the assessment form.

7. Completed forms submit via online form.

8. SAWC receives a report detailing all non-compliance areas.

9. Reminders are sent for missing submissions; non-receipt triggers follow-up per SOP 7 after one month.

10. SAWC reviews remedial plans for non-compliant members; the Board may recommend membership termination if no plans were submitted.

11. Multi-site members should refer to SOP 4 for guidance on assessment scope.
